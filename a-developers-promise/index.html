A Developer's Promise

A promise that means if I publish a project, I keep it updated until there's demand for it.

If it's a Minecraft mod or pack, I will keep it up-to-date until there are people who need it or shows interest for it.

If it's a different project like a JavaScript library for exapmle, I will keep it updated until I use it or it become almost bugless.
